The dataset used for these visualizations is from an Amazon Customer Behavior Survey. We were interested in the average Amazon shopper's behavior and wondered if it differs by gender.

  • Shopping Satisfaction: How satisfied are you with your overall shopping experience on Amazon? 1-5. 1 being not satisfied, 5 being very satisfied
  • Browsing Frequency: How often do you browse Amazon's website or app? 1-4. Rarely, Few times a month, Few times a week, Multiple times a day
  • Purchase Frequency: How frequently do you make purchases on Amazon? Multiple times a week, Once a week, Few times a month, Once a month, Less than once a month

This is user shopping satisfaction by gender.
